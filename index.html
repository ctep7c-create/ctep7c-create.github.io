My name is Chanté Hearn and here you will find a selection of my work, projects and accomplishments. I work as the Assistant Director for the International Center for Supplemental Instruction, and as an Academic Support Coordinator at the University of Missouri-Kansas City. Enjoy exploring my digital portfolio!

Purpose & Goals

I aim to spark creativity, interdependence, and joy through my work. By embracing diversity of thought and experience, I strive to make a positive difference in the world. My goal is to cultivate learning environments that empower individuals to thrive and contribute their unique talents to our collective growth and understanding. In higher education, I am inspired by the opportunity to connect with adults from diverse backgrounds and fields, supporting their development in e-learning and digital literacy.
